About me

I am an Assistant Professor in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at Wichita State University, Wichita, KS. From 2015 to 2017, I was a postdoctoral researcher at the Pennsylvania State University, University Park, PA. I received the Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering from the Georgia Institute of Technology, Atlanta, GA, in 2015, and the Engineering degree from Supélec, Gif-sur-Yvette, France in 2011.

My research is supported by an NSF-CAREER award, an NSF-CRII award, an NSF-CIF Collaborative Research award, and an NSF Convergence Accelerator award.
